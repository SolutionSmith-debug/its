Pile Installation:
Piles.
Subcontractor is responsible for providing all labor, equipment and to install and perform the work listed below in accordance with the Contract Documents so that the system is completely functional and operational.
Provide post driving equipment, and labor to perform installation of all posts including racking posts, CAB system posts, and inverter rack posts.
Pile Summary
Posts sizes and embedment depths in accordance with the Contract Documents.
The Subcontractor is to use proper means and methods for work performed under this Scope of Work to ensure that piles are driven in the correct locations with consideration to alerting site supervisor of layout problems before driving piles.
Pile Installation:
Installation of piles to meet racking tolerances per racking vendor Installation Manual and the Contract Document requirements.
Special consideration is required by subcontractor to install piles using the above ground tolerances provided by racking vendor and contract documents to account for grade changes and provide array rows that are gradual in angular changes and of flow like appearance.
Subcontractor is responsible for all other pile layout and location verification before installing piles. Subcontractor is responsible to alert the site supervisor of any layout discrepancies before installing piles.
Subcontractor to keep a detailed map of piles installed, and refusals found. This map to be turned over to Contractor on a daily basis.
Refusals shall be remediated per the Racking Manufacturer’s refusals plan and specifications. Refusal remediation shall be performed in a timely manner and per the included unit rate.
